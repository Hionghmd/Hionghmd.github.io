英语口语 Day_1

1.my family has always been caring and loving;

2.we barely made enough money for a comfortable living.

3.keep one’s float 维持某人的生计

4.janitorial 清洁的

5.all the while 一直以来

6.under my belt 已有的成就

example： I have got three jobs under my belt.

7.siblings 兄弟姐妹

8.I answered with cheesy jokes. 我用了烂俗的玩笑回答

9.cafeteria 食堂

10.work sent me a little curveball too. 工作方面也出现了一些问题

11.at my usual late shift 夜班

12.smile change to a frown 皱眉头

13.make a career out of it somehow 以此作为一份职业

14.sportscaster 体育比赛解说员

15.see a path in my life 看到我的人生方向

16.mixed results 喜忧参半

17.hang out with sb 与某人闲逛

18.introduce me in front of entire class 在全班面前介绍我

Keep believing that everthing will be alrigth.

Day_2

1.constant frustration

2.attentionseeker 渴求受到关注的人

3.no one in his right mind would … 没有任何一个头脑正常的人会

4.toddlers 蹒跚步行走的人；学步的幼儿

5.selfacceptance 自我接纳

6.outcast 被抛弃的人

7.impairments or deformations 缺陷或畸形

8.It’s easier to survive laughing than suffering

9.derogatory 贬义的

10.chic 漂亮的，时髦的

11.plastic surgery 整形手术

P

长句子

  • I‘ll have a story about my constant frustration.

  • It’s not i am an attentinseeker .我并不是一个渴求受到关注的人。

  • No one in his right mind would call me pretty.没有任何一个头脑正常的人会夸我漂亮。

  • A genetic lottery as they say,in this case i haven’t got a single number called out.

​ 如果以基因彩票举例，那么我一个号码都没中。（可以用来表示倒霉）

  • But I have not always had this easy attitude toward myself.

    但我也不总是这么积极乐观。

  • People are always cruel towards who are weak and embarrass and praise those who are strong and live in peace with their situation.
